# [[Insaniyat unreleased\|Insaniyat unreleased]]


Qismat ha kharab sab mere ha khelaf
Ankhn mere numb ha baaki ha insaaf

Khuda jane kisne kiya kya gunaah
wohi dega sabko unka saza

paisa dekha do toh bekega insaan
thoda daam badha aur kharidle iman

paisa ke ha bhuuk
sabke dil mai pyar yahi sabse bada ek jhoot

sabko chaheye zindagi mai fame
isiliye banalete zindagi ko game

dard ka asar mere dil p huwa
rooh kaap uthe jab khoof aya ke
	chala gaya wo jisse sacche dil se pyar ha huwa

galat karke sab bolenge ke tere galti hai
bharosa kiya yahi tere galti hai

toh phir insaniyat kaha ha batao?
insaniyat ko parde se chupao.

koi nai tune kiya tujhe be dena hoga khuda ko jawab
tu kon ha joh lega mere har ek gunah k hisab
sun mere baat ko dek apne haal ko
tu toh bhai insaan he nahi

ander se haiwan
qadr kar sabke
karega khuda jo tu karega

yaad ha mujhe mere bure din wo
bhul gaya sabkuch bhul gaya mai
baat yaad ha jo dil p lage
bhulunga nahi jo mujhpe guzre.
